INSPIRE Capstone - Mission Statement

INSPIRE is a Davenport West High School Initiative dedicated to creating a school-wide culture where students identify, select, and pursue career pathways and develop necessary skills that lead to post-secondary career and / or college opportunities. INSPIRE is an extension of the nationally recognized career programs at West High School.

Our unique capstone class unites Advanced Manufacturing, Business, Computer Science, and Engineering career pathways. It is important to note that PLTW and STEM Innovator influence our daytime curriculum. We are more than robots. Our two feature capstone projects are FRC Robotics and our Clean Water for Kenya Initiative.
